About Me

Physicist — Data Scientist — Problem Solver

  • Download CV

From Theoretical Physics to Data Science

Former physicist, with a PhD in theoretical physics and applied mathematics, I have built up considerable experience in research, ranging from black holes and quantum computing to communication through the atmosphere and hydrodynamics.

I'm now transitioning towards the world of data. With a deep understanding of data analysis, modeling, machine and deep learning, I love to "give data a voice", explore patterns hidden from our eyes, and transform them into interpretable and operational levers.

AI for a Better World

I am particularly fascinated by AI applications in healthcare, pharmaceutical research, and oncology, where turning data into meaningful insights can accelerate drug discovery, improve diagnosis, and enable more personalized treatments. I am convinced that artificial intelligence can help us make tremendous progress and improve human life.

A Mindset Built on Reasoning

Over the years, I have developed a sharp ability to analyze and resolve complex problems. I have broadened my expertise to include machine learning, deep learning, and other advanced analytical tools. But what matters the most is reasoning — a skill that extends much beyond physics and research and applies to any domain.

Data are everywhere. They just need us to unlock their secrets.
